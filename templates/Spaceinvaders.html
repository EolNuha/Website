Space Invaders

What is Space Invaders?

Space invaders is like the classic '80s space invaders game where you have a spaceship and you shoot aliens and avoid asteroids.
To play this game you use the left and right arrows in order to move left and right, and the space bar to shoot bullets to hit the aliens.
With each level the spaceship changes and you can shoot more bullets, but also the number of aliens and asteroid rises.
The game has six normal levels and two boss levels. If you manage to defeat the second boss you'll enter an infinite level where you can play as long as you like until you lose your lives.

Link to download the application to play the game!

In the downloaded file you'll have two folders: the app and the photos folder. In the photos folder are all the game assets needed to play the game.
For the game to work the app and the photos folder need to be in the same directory.
The downloaded file will come in the form of a zip file but just extract it to a normal file.
