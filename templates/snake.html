What is Snake Game?

Snake Game is the recreation of the classic game on our dad's nokia phones if you've managed to catch that time.
To play this game use all the keys to move forward, backwards, left, and right.
So in this game there's not really anything different, there's just one level and that is until your snake gets to big to play. So that is kinda like your victory.
