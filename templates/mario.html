Super Mario Bros

What is Super Mario Bros?

Super Mario Bros is the recreation of the old Super Mario Bros level 1. Its exactly like that.
You can eat the mushroom to get bigger and gain one extra life. You have to jump and avoid the monsters and the turtle.
To play this game use the up button or the letter 'a' to jump, the left and right arrows to move left and right. The game is on a timer, it means that after 5 minutes if you don't complete the level than you will lose.
Also the more coins you make its better for your score on the game.

Link to download the application to play the game!

The downloaded file will come in the form of a zip file but just extract it to a normal file.
